# [COG-001] Lotus Synthesis

> [!NOTE]
> **The "Hook":** A reasoning engine for high-ambiguity or ethical problems that forces reconciliation of contradictions.

## Metadata
- **ID:** COG-001
- **Title:** Lotus Synthesis
- **Paradigm:** Dialectic Reasoning, Ethical Analysis
- **Integration Cost:** Low (Prompt Wrapper)
- **Status:** Operational

## Problem Domain
Linear logic often fails when faced with complex, multi-layered ethical dilemmas or highly ambiguous scenarios where "Correct" and "Incorrect" are subjective.

## The Paradigm
The Lotus Synthesis forces the agent through a 5-stage dialectic process:
1. **The Seed:** State the core contradiction or paradox.
2. **The Bloom:** Expand on the conflicting viewpoints without judgment.
3. **The Mirror:** Identify the underlying shared values between the conflicts.
4. **The Synthesis:** Propose a solution that integrates the truths of both sides.
5. **The Fragrance:** Summarize the wisdom gained from the reconciliation.

## Killer App
**Conflict Resolution:** Ideal for agents acting as mediators, strategic advisors, or ethical auditors.
